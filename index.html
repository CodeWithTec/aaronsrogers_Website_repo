About My Ministry

I am Aaron S. Rogers, a gospel musician called to minister through songs of deliverance, worship, and praise.

With over a decade of music ministry, my heart is to see lives transformed by the power of God's word in melody. Every song carries a message of hope for the broken, healing for the sick, and joy for the weary.
